Key Features

  • Inventory Management: Easily manage your snack inventory by adding, editing, and removing items. Track stock levels in real-time and make adjustments seamlessly.
  • Sales Tracking: Monitor sales and billing activities with automatic inventory updates, including order and delivery dates, and payment statuses.
  • Delivery Management: Track undelivered quantities and schedule timely reminders for upcoming deliveries.
  • Customer Management: Organize your customer database and link customers to specific events or transactions easily.
  • Event-Based Organization: Manage multiple events and monitor the stock needed for each occasion.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Enjoy a simple yet powerful design that offers essential tools without overwhelming complexity.
  • Customizable Notifications: Schedule and customize notifications for various events, ensuring you stay informed.
  • Data Backup and Restoration: Easily create backups of your records to protect your data.
  • Offline Access: Manage inventory and track sales even without an internet connection.

Key Features

  • Puzzle Challenges: Solve increasingly difficult puzzles to progress through the game.
  • Cloud Save: Your game progress is saved to Firestore when an internet connection is available, ensuring you can resume your game seamlessly across sessions and devices.
  • Local Storage Backup: When offline, your game progress is stored locally, and synchronization occurs once connectivity is restored.
  • Google Login Integration: Authenticate using Google Login to securely save and restore your progress and facilitate multiplayer features.
